Why can't I book an exam?

We cannot show any exams for you to book. This may be because you need to:

  • arrange a resit (if you failed your last exam or cancelled it within 21 days of the booked date)
  • complete Part A of your oral exam
  • reapply for your Notice of Eligibility (NoE)

Arranging a resit

Check your email inbox for your exam result or cancellation confirmation email. The emaiI we sent you contains instructions on how you can apply and pay for a resit.

Completing the Part A exam

Some Maritime and Coastguard Agency (MCA) qualifications require passing the oral proctored Part A exam before Part B can be booked.

If you need to take Part A of the exam, we will have sent you instructions on how to take Part A, along with login details, to your NoE email address.

When you have passed Part A of the oral exam, you will be sent a link to book Part B. You must pass Part B within 1 year of passing Part A.

Reapplying for your NoE

An NoE is valid for 5 years. If your NoE has expired, you must apply for a new NoE before booking an exam. An NoE is valid for 5 years. If yours has expired, you need to get a new NoE by reapplying for a certificate of competency before you can book an exam.
